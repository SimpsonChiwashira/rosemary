About Us

Who is Rosemary Healthcare? How can we help?

What is our mission statement?

It is to empower individuals with diverse abilities to live fulfilling lives by providing personalized, compassionate support within a nurturing and inclusive community. We are dedicated to fostering independence, dignity, and meaningful connections, ensuring each person's journey is respected and celebrated.

Local Authority Safeguarding

We have polices and procedures for the sharing of safeguarding information amongst other forms of information that might be shared with other agencies and professionals. These are written to comply with confidentiality principles and data protection laws.

The organisation develops its policies and procedures in line with our local Safeguarding Adults Boards (SABs) recommendation and guidance with relevant documentation for raising alerts and staff training.

Policy Statement

The safeguarding policy sets out the following:

  • Principles and values underlying our approach to the safeguarding of people receiving care.
  • Ways in which rosemary healthcare conducts its safeguarding policies.
  • Steps taken to avoid abuse.
